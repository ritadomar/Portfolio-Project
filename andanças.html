Festival Andanças '21

Overview

The goal this week was to develop a website that reflected what Andanças is about, while also keeping the design clean and fresh.

The Challenge

We were challenged to choose a festival/event and develop a microsite for its next edition, focusing on the appearance of the website before and during the event.

The Festival

Andanças is a yearly Dances of the World festival, but it isn’t just that. Near the Póvoa e Meada’s Dam, the festival drinks from the surrounding nature and scenery, becoming an epicenter for creativity, self-expression, and all kinds of culture.
